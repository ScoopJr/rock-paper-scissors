Console Rock Paper Scissor

A simple implementation of the classic game Rock, Paper, Scissors. Follow the instructions below to play the game.

  1. To play the game, open the console (How to).
  2. Click the Start button below.
  3. Play best of 5 rounds against the computer.
  4. If you want to play again, click Play Again.
  5. A running total will be kept of all rounds played.
